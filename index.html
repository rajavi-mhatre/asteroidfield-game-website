Welcome to AsteroidField!


How to play:


Our astronauts are in danger! Help them navigate their spaceship through a deadly asteroid belt!
This game tests your ability to avoid obstacles at super high speeds.
The longer you survive, the harder it gets. To move, simply use the ↑ ↓ keys on your keyboard. Enjoy!
